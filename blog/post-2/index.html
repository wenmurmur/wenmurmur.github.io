VB classic回應封包設HttpOnly

By Wen 17 March 2021

iis設定Cookie HttpOnly

在web.config已經增加下面，卻還是沒效

<system.web>
     <httpCookies httpOnlyCookies="true"/>
</system.web>

查到VB classic可以在<system.webServer>增加rewrite module

<system.webServer>
    <rewrite> ​
        <outboundRules> ​
            <rule name="Add HttpOnly" preCondition="No HttpOnly"> ​
                <match serverVariable="RESPONSE_Set_Cookie" pattern=".*" negate="false" /> ​
                <action type="Rewrite" value="{R:0}; HttpOnly" /> ​
                <conditions> ​
                </conditions> ​
            </rule> ​
            <preConditions> ​
                <preCondition name="No HttpOnly"> ​
                    <add input="{RESPONSE_Set_Cookie}" pattern="." /> ​
                    <add input="{RESPONSE_Set_Cookie}" pattern="; HttpOnly" negate="true" /> ​
                </preCondition> ​
            </preConditions> ​
        </outboundRules> ​
    </rewrite>
</system.webServer>

增加rewrite module前要先下載 https://www.iis.net/downloads/microsoft/url-rewrite

可以使用Free Web Platform Installer或拉到下面Download URL Rewrite Module 2.1選擇版本

此版本 Works With: IIS 7, IIS 7.5, IIS 8, IIS 8.5, IIS 10

只要是正版OS都可以下載

※rewrite module從IIS 7開始支援
